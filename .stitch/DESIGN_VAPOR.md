# Design System: Vapor Logic (Cyber-Futurist)

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
**Creative North Star: "The Synthetic Architect"**
Designed for developers and technical power users. It emphasizes speed, density, and precision. The UI feels like an orbital command center or a high-end IDE.

---

## 2. Colors & Surface Philosophy
High-contrast shadows and neon highlights.
- **Surface:** `#060812` (Near Black)
- **Primary:** `#00f5ff` (Electric Cyan)
- **Secondary:** `#7000ff` (Neon Violet)
- **Border:** 1px Gradient (`cyan` to `transparent`)

---

## 3. Typography
- **Headlines:** JetBrains Mono (700)
- **Body:** Inter (400)
- **Monospace Elements:** Fira Code (with ligatures)

---

## 4. Components
- **Buttons:** 1px solid Primary border, neon outer glow.
- **Cards:** Heavy `backdrop-blur` (64px), semi-transparent indigo fill.
- **Inputs:** Terminal-style prompts with blinking underscore cursors.
