# Design System: Scholar Soft (Humanist AI)

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
**Creative North Star: "The Modern Library"**
Focuses on deep focus, reading, and scholarly trust. It should feel as premium as an expensive physical notebook or a leather-bound library.

---

## 2. Colors & Surface Philosophy
Soft whites and organic shadows.
- **Surface:** `#fcfaf5` (Warm Bone)
- **Primary:** `#064e3b` (Forest Green)
- **Secondary:** `#d97706` (Burnt Sienna)
- **Border:** Tonal shadows (no hard lines)

---

## 3. Typography
- **Headlines:** Fraunces (700) - A modern, soft serif.
- **Body:** Inter (400)
- **Labels:** Archivo (600)

---

## 4. Components
- **Buttons:** Solid Forest Green with gold text. 0.25rem roundness.
- **Cards:** Diffused ambient shadows (X: 0, Y: 12, Blur: 24, Color: `rgba(90, 70, 50, 0.05)`).
- **Inputs:** Minimalist with a soft `surface_container` fill and `on_surface` labels.
